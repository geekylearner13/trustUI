Harmony Where Every Child Shines Brightly

A Skill Development Studio born from a mother's dream, supported by the Inner Wheel Club of Survey Park Stars

"Every child deserves the opportunity to discover their unique gifts and shine in their own beautiful way."

A Vision Rooted in Love and Resilience

Dr. Suman Kumari's journey began with a simple yet profound realization: every child with Autism Spectrum Disorder possesses unique talents waiting to be discovered and nurtured.

Born from personal experience and unwavering determination, Harmony represents more than just a skill development center—it's a beacon of hope, understanding, and endless possibility.

With the generous support of the Inner Wheel Club of Survey Park Stars, we've created a space where children can explore, learn, and flourish in an environment designed specifically for their needs.

Dr. Suman Kumari

Founder & Visionary
