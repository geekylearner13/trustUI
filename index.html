Brahmdeo Sah Memorial Trust

Honoring a Legacy, Empowering Lives

Brahmdeo Sah Memorial Trust was founded in loving memory of Brahmdeo Sah, a visionary whose life was dedicated to compassion, service, and uplifting those in need. His unwavering commitment to social welfare, education, and community development continues to inspire generations.

What we focus on

Education for All

Education is the cornerstone of progress. We are committed to providing quality learning opportunities to children and youth, regardless of their socio-economic background. Through scholarships, mentorship, and skill development programs, we empower individuals to build a brighter future.

Accessible Healthcare

Health is a fundamental right, not a privilege. Our trust works to ensure that everyone, especially underserved communities, has access to essential medical services, preventive care, and health awareness programs. We strive to promote well-being and save lives through compassionate healthcare initiatives.

Social Welfare

A thriving society is built on kindness and collective strength. We actively engage in social welfare programs that address key community challenges, including poverty alleviation, disaster relief, and civic engagement. Through meaningful action, we aim to uplift lives and bring about positive change
